]]> Urban Feed Co https://winstonford.github.io/urban-feed-co/ Fri, 19 Apr 2019 23:16:09 +0000 https://winstonford.github.io/?p=75 Urban Feed Co is a project to produce sustainable livestock feed from insects that eat wide variety of nutrient wastes. These insects are called black soldier flies, or hermetic illucens, and while native to the tropics, they now live on every inhabited continent. The insect larvae are 30-50% protein. They contain lauric acid, also a major constituent in coconut oil, which kills harmful pathogens like bacteria, viruses and fungi. For more info, visit UrbanFeedCo.com or checkout the timeline.
